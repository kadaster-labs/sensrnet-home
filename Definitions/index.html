    Definitions

    These definitions of fields are part of the SensRNet datamodel. If availible, we've used the definitions of OGC's SensorThingsAPI; the other definitions are our own.

    Common Types

    Field: AggregateId ✅
  1. Definition (EN): Identification number of the Aggregate
  2. Definitie (NL): Identificatienummer van de gegevensgroep
  3. NL: Aggregatie identificatie
